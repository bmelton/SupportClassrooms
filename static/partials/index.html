Support Classrooms

Support Classrooms is a 501(c)(3) designed to aid and further education and encourage stronger relationships between parents, teachers and education faculty.

Teachers spend as much as $1,000 out of their own pockets on necessity items including school supplies, field trips, clothing for children in need, and food and snacks.

Collectively, teachers spend up to $1.3 billion on these sorts of supplies. Support Classrooms aims to end this.
